About us

Welcome to Tuku Creations, where passion meets craftsmanship. As a family of artists with over two decades of experience in restoration and art production, we take pride in our dedication to preserving the beauty of the past while creating stunning pieces for the future. Specializing in woodwork restoration, papery mixed media art, and jewelry crafting, our team combines traditional techniques with innovative approaches to bring your vision to life. Whether it's breathing new life into antique furniture, crafting unique paper-based artworks, or designing exquisite jewelry pieces, Tuku Creations is your trusted partner in the world of artistry and restoration. Join us on a journey where creativity knows no bounds, and every creation tells a story
